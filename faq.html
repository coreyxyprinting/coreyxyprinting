FAQ

What file types do you accept?

STL and 3MF preferred. OBJ accepted.

What materials do you offer?

PLA and PETG standard. TPU by request.

Do you design parts?

No—CoreyXYPrinting prints customer-supplied designs only.

Can you print copyrighted models?

Only if you own the rights or have permission to print the model.

How long does it take?

Most small jobs are completed within 1–3 business days after payment.

Do you ship?

Yes, or local pickup if arranged.
