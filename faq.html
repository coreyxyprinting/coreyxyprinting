Quick answers

FAQ

Here are the most common questions about ordering prints from your designs.

Ordering

What file types do you accept?

I prefer STL and 3MF. OBJ is also ok. If you have multiple files, upload a ZIP and explain what each part is.

Do you create designs?

Right now I’m focused on printing customer-supplied designs. If you already have a model, I can print it.

What materials can you print?

PLA and PETG by default. TPU available by request depending on the part.

What’s typical turnaround?

Most jobs are 1–3 business days after payment, depending on size/complexity and current queue.

Quality & finishing

Can you improve strength?

Yes—choosing PETG, adjusting wall count, infill, and orientation can improve strength. If you select “Not sure,” I’ll recommend settings.

Do you remove supports and clean parts?

Yes. I remove supports, clean edges, and do a quick quality check before delivery.

Still have a question?

Send a note and I’ll reply quickly.
